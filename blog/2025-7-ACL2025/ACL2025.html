♣ Cultural Bias Matters: A Cross-Cultural Benchmark Dataset and Sentiment-Enriched Model for Understanding Multimodal Metaphors

This paper addresses the challenge of understanding multimodal metaphors across different cultures. It introduces a new benchmark dataset that captures metaphorical expressions in both text and images, annotated for cultural context and sentiment polarity. The authors argue that cultural bias plays a significant role in how metaphors are interpreted and propose a sentiment-enriched model that leverages both visual and textual modalities.

Key contributions:

  • A large-scale cross-cultural multimodal metaphor dataset with sentiment annotations.
  • Analysis showing how metaphor interpretation varies significantly across cultures.
  • A multimodal transformer-based architecture enriched with sentiment features for improved metaphor understanding.

Experiments demonstrate that incorporating sentiment and cultural context improves metaphor detection and interpretation accuracy, especially for non-Western datasets. This research paves the way for culturally-aware multimodal NLP systems.
