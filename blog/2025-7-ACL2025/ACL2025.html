In this short blog post, I will highlight one of the most interesting papers presented in ACL \(2025\).

♣ Cultural Bias Matters: A Cross-Cultural Benchmark Dataset and Sentiment-Enriched Model for Understanding Multimodal Metaphors

This paper tackles the underexplored challenge of understanding multimodal metaphors across different cultural contexts. The authors propose a large-scale cross-cultural benchmark dataset combining **visual and textual metaphors** from multiple languages and cultures. They also introduce a **sentiment-enriched multimodal metaphor understanding model** that integrates emotional cues with cross-cultural semantics.

Key contributions include:

  • Creation of a **benchmark dataset** covering multiple cultures for multimodal metaphor interpretation.
  • Introduction of a **sentiment-enriched fusion model** for better metaphor understanding.
  • Extensive evaluation demonstrating significant improvements over state-of-the-art multimodal models.

This work highlights the importance of **cultural bias** in metaphor comprehension and provides tools and benchmarks for more inclusive NLP systems.
